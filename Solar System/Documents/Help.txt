Solar System v1.02 help - by: Peter Swinkels, ***2022***

Key(s):		Function:
+/-		Adjust speed.
Arrows		Adjust the camera's position.
F1		Program information.
Home/End	Zoom in/out.
Page Down/Up	Tilt the ecliptic plane.
Pause		Pause all movements.

Mouse:		Function:
Left Button	Adjust the camera's position.
